WHO ARE THE PLANETEERS.

In Spring 2017, a number of students from the New York City College of Technology in Downtown Brooklyn had begun to notice that little was being done to encourage recycling or promote sustainable living on our campus. Insufficient recycling bins, the Co-mingling of trash, unmaintained green spaces, and the lack of knowledge and concern about environmental issues by a vast majority of students were the main problems that motivated us to seek change. The shared passion for environmental justice made the students assemble into a green task force which is now formally known as the PLANETEERS.

By working together, we believe we can support the environment by helping raise awareness, providing tools for change and motivation, and by actively participating in projects that have an immediate impact on the environment such as collecting recyclable items, gardening. The club meets once a month to discuss projects, events, and issues concerning the environment that affects us on a local, national, and global level. We are constantly working towards developing solutions to problems we are facing on campus and in the community.


WHAT WE DO

The City Tech Planeteers are a green task force whose mission is to raise awareness about the effects of pollution and Climate Change and to encourage recycling, energy and water conservation. We believe the key to saving our planet is through sustainable living.
